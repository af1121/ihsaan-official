The challenge

You aced your exams while fasting in Ramadan. Kept your prayers through late library nights, carried your family's hopes, and did the hard work. Yet for too many Muslims, rising educational success in Muslim communities isn't translating into the job market.

The gap shows up in real life.

  • Young Muslim unemployment is 13%, compared with 9% nationally.
  • Only 6% reach senior professional roles, compared with 10% overall.
  • Muslims are twice as likely to live in deprived areas.
  • Career paths can clash with faith, values, and ethical boundaries.

The potential we're unlocking

The issue isn't talent or ambition. It's the barriers holding people back. Yet this same community is among the youngest in the country, full of potential waiting to be realised. IHSAAN is dedicated to unlocking that energy, ambition, and untapped talent by providing:

  • Employer access
  • Mentorship and role models
  • Faith-conscious career support
