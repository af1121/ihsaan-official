Connecting Muslim students with leading employers, role models, and career pathways.

What Are Career Conferences?

Our career conferences are IHSAAN's flagship programme — large-scale, professionally organised events that bring Muslim students face-to-face with employers, industry leaders, and successful professionals. Held at universities and venues across the UK and Ireland, these conferences give students the chance to explore career paths, attend skills workshops, and build the networks that will shape their futures.

What to Expect

  • Keynote talks from professionals across finance, law, technology, healthcare, consulting, and more
  • Employer exhibition stands where you can discover roles, internships, and graduate schemes
  • Panel discussions on navigating faith and career, social mobility, and industry-specific insights
  • Practical workshops on CVs, interviews, networking, and personal branding
  • Networking sessions with professionals who understand the unique journey of Muslim graduates

Why Attend?

Our conferences are designed to be more than just careers fairs. They are spaces where Muslim students feel seen, supported, and inspired. You will leave with practical tools, real connections, and a clearer sense of direction — whether you are in your first year or about to graduate.
