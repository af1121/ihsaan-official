Partner With Us

We partner with employers, professional networks, and community organisations who share our commitment to social mobility and diversity. As an IHSAAN partner, you gain access to a pipeline of ambitious, values-driven Muslim talent while supporting meaningful change.

Partnership opportunities include:

  • Sponsoring or hosting a career conference at your organisation
  • Providing mentors through our mentorship programmes
  • Sharing internship, graduate, and entry-level roles via our Opportunities Tracker
  • Co-delivering workshops, panel discussions, or skills sessions
