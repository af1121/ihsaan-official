We take the energy of a competitive hackathon and point it at problems that actually matter — challenges set by real charities, councils, and companies who need your skills.

What makes IHSAAN Hackathons different?

We do everything a great hackathon does — and use those same skills to build something that genuinely helps others and leaves a lasting mark.

Learning worth years

Masterclasses that transform you in minutes, not months.

Practical workshops in entrepreneurship, pitching, and communication — leaving every participant with real insight and confidence within a 40-minute session, not just a certificate.

Impact that scales

Real organisations. Real problems. Real change.
